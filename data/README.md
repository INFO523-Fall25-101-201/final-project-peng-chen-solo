# Codebook for [chosen] Dataset

## Variable Names and Descriptions:

-   **PC1**: Genetic background, calculated using SNP data
-   **PC2**: Genetic background, calculated using SNP data
-   **Male**: gender, 0: female, 1: male
-   **BMI**: body mass index
-   **Age**: age
-   **OSA**: target variable, obstructive sleep apnea
-   **APOE_e4**: genotype of APOE e4 allele
-   **f_25887_2_0**: volume of grey matter in Hippocampus (right, mm3)
-   **f_25886_2_0**: Volume of grey matter in Hippocampus (left)
-   **f_25003_2_0**: Volume of ventricular cerebrospinal fluid (normalised for head size)
-   **f_25004_2_0**: Volume of ventricular cerebrospinal fluid
-   **f_25001_2_0**: Volume of peripheral cortical grey matter (normalised for head size)
-   **f_25002_2_0**: Volume of peripheral cortical grey matter
-   **f_25005_2_0**: Volume of grey matter (normalised for head size)
-   **f_25006_2_0**: Volume of grey matter
-   **f_25007_2_0**: Volume of white matter (normalised for head size)
-   **f_25008_2_0**: Volume of white matter
-   **f_25009_2_0**: Volume of brain, grey+white matter (normalised for head size)
-   **f_25010_2_0**: Volume of brain, grey+white matter
-   **f_25012_2_0**: Volume of thalamus (right)
-   **f_25011_2_0**: Volume of thalamus (left)
-   **f_25016_2_0**: Volume of accumbens (left)
-   **f_25015_2_0**: Volume of accumbens (right)
-   **f_25018_2_0**: Volume of amygdala (left)
-   **f_25017_2_0**: Volume of amygdala (right)
-   **f_25020_2_0**: Volume of caudate (left)
-   **f_25019_2_0**: Volume of caudate (right)
-   **f_25014_2_0**: Volume of hippocampus (left)
-   **f_25013_2_0**: Volume of hippocampus (right)
-   **f_25022_2_0**: Volume of pallidum (left)
-   **f_25021_2_0**: Volume of pallidum (right)
-   **f_25024_2_0**: Volume of putamen (left)
-   **f_25023_2_0**: Volume of putamen (right)
